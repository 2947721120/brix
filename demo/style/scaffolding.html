混入函数

Brix Style提供了许多Mixins来加速Brix组件的开发。

暂定有以下mixin

  1. .border-radius (@radius)
  2. .opacity (@opacity)
  3. .box-shadow (@shadow)
  4. .transition (@transition)
  5. #gradient > .vertical (@startColor: #555, @endColor: #333)
  6. #gradient > .striped (@color, @angle: -45deg)
  7. .ie7-inline-block ()
  8. .reset-filter ()
  9. .buttonBackground (@startColor, @endColor)
  10. .formFieldState (@textColor: #555, @borderColor: #ccc, @backgroundColor: #f5f5f5)
  11. #popoverArrow > .bottom (@arrowWidth: 5px, @color: @black)
  12. #popoverArrow > .right (@arrowWidth: 5px, @color: @black)
  13. #popoverArrow > .top (@arrowWidth: 5px, @color: @black)
  14. #popoverArrow > .left (@arrowWidth: 5px, @color: @black)
  15. #grid > .core (@gridColumnWidth, @gridGutterWidth)
  16. #grid > .fluid (@fluidGridColumnWidth, @fluidGridGutterWidth)
  17. #grid > .input (@gridColumnWidth, @gridGutterWidth)
